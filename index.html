I am currently a Research Fellow at Mathematics Institute, University of Warwick, supervised by Prof. Oleg Pikhurko.

I got my Ph.D. degree in 2022 from the Department of Mathematics, Statistics, and Computer Science, University of Illinois at Chicago (UIC). My advisor was Prof. Dhruv Mubayi.

I got my B.S. degree in 2018 from the School of Mathematical Sciences, University of Science and Technology of China (USTC). My undergraduate advisor was Prof. Jie Ma.

Research Interests:

Extremal Combinatorics, Theoretical Computer Science, Complexity Theory.
